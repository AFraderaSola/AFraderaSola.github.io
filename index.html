About

I am a PhD candidate in Quantitative Proteomics in the Institute of Molecular Biology. My research interests focus on bioinformatics, especially omics data and its statistical analysis. My work revolves around analyzing NGS-based genomics and quantitative mass spectrometry-based proteomics data. I consider myself both collaborative and adaptable. I am always eager to tackle new and different projects. I am very enthusiastic to share and develop my data visualization skills as a means towards better science communication. I am an open-minded person and enjoy having great relationships with my colleagues.

Interactive CV network
Do you want to make your CV shine? Learn how to create a network plot (as an image or as an interactive .html) based on the entries on a standard CV with a tutorial based on my own résumé.
RBP interactome characterization in yeast
Main PhD project - RNA binding proteins (RBPs) were selected according to their involvement in RNA pathways and immunoprecipitated. This allowed us to create a function-based network that enabled the assignment of new functionalities on novel and known RBPs. This work was recently presented at CSAMA 2022.
Slides
Scoring function development for RNA-Seq differential expression assessment
Main M.Sc project - I developed a scoring function around three R-based RNA-Seq differential expression packages (DESeq2, edgeR, limma+voom). The main goal was to assess ow well packages perform and compare the their results in order to maximize (or restric, depenging on your criteria) the number of detected DEGs.

June, 2022 In PLoS Pathogens
A novel SNF2 ATPase complex in Trypanosoma brucei with a role in H2A.Z-mediated chromatin remodeling

ChIP-Seq data was analyzed to characterize the novel SNF2 ATPase in Trypanosoma brucei. Differential H2A.Z deposition was detected after RNAi depletion (against no depletion control) and visualized with genome tracks.

Differential gene expression and gene ontologies associated with increasing water-stress in leaf and root transcriptomes of perennial ryegrass (Lolium perenne)
